🧪 Kuviyam Extension Test Page

Testing Instructions:

Step 1: Open DevTools (F12) and check the Console tab
Step 2: Look for this message: [Kuviyam] Content script loaded
Step 3: Press Alt+Shift+P to toggle the panel
Step 4: OR click the extension icon and press 📌 Open Panel
Expected Result: A floating panel should appear with "KUVIYAM" header

Troubleshooting:

  • If no console message appears → Content script not injected
  • Check edge://extensions → Ensure Kuviyam is enabled
  • Click "Reload" on the extension
  • Refresh this page
  • Check for errors in the extension's service worker console
